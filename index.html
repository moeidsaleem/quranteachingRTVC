Quran tafseer

Learn Tafsir online by learning meanings of the words of the Holy Quran. This Course will allow you to understand the meanings and commands of Allah S.W.T. This is not to develop scholarly views and will not lead you to be a scholar. This course will be helping in developing general understanding of Ahkam, Commands of Allah Tala’l. Tafsir of the Qur’an is the most important science of Quran. All matters concerning the Islamic way of life are connected to it in one sense or another since the right application of Islam is based on proper understanding of the guidance from Allah. Without Tafsir there would be no right understanding of various passages of the Qur’an. Learning Tafsir involves knowledge of basic Arabic grammar, Usul-al-Tafsir, and Ilmul Balagah.
